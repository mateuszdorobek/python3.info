Rationale
---------
* ``df.at[]``
* ``df.loc[]``
* ``df.iat[]``
* ``df.iloc[]``
* ``iat`` and ``iloc`` takes ``int`` as argument
* ``at`` and ``loc`` takes ``str`` as argument
