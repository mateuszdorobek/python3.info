Dragon ADR Damage Take
======================


Rationale
---------
* ADR - Architecture Design Records
* Make DMG points damage to the dragon


Possibilities
-------------
>>> dragon.take_damage(DMG)  # good

>>> dragon.receive_damage(DMG)

>>> dragon.wound(DMG)

>>> dragon.hurt(DMG)

>>> dragon.hit(DMG)

>>> dragon.damage(DMG)

>>> dragon.hurt_self(DMG)

>>> dragon - DMG

>>> dragon.__sub__(DMG)

>>> dragon.receive_damage(DMG)
